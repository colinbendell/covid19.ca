COVID-19 Status

As of 3:15PM PT yesterday, over 1.3 million Canadians have tested positive for COVID-19. Sadly, 25 thousand have died.

In the last day, 5.7 thousand new infections were reported and 64 deaths. This is trending downwards.

Fortunately, 18.8 million doses of the vaccine have been administered. For those 12 years and older, 52.3% have their 1st shot and 4.3% are fully vaccinated!

Vaccination is speeding up, but there are 1.6 million doses in the freezer. It currently takes ~8 days for new deliveries to get into arms. At the current rate, all Canadians aged 12+ should have the first dose by Jun-29 and fully vaccinated by Aug-17. There is no approved vaccine for children aged 11 or younger.
